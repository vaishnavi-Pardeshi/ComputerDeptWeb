HOD's Message

Welcome to Department of Computer Engineering at GPP! In this era of Cyber and digital world, education is always focusing on quality of education. GPP being leader among Polytechnics across the country, we at Department of Computer Engineering strives continuously for improvement and enhancement of quality in every aspect. Department of Computer Engineering works diligently to realize mission of providing latest and modern curricula, deployment of latest tools and technology, inculcate positive-attitude, self-learning and problem solving skills among students, and to train faculty with state-of-art knowledge and skills.

Department's Vision & Mission

"To develop innovative, dynamic, and proficient computer engineers to fulfill need of stakeholders"

  • Design and develop latest and modern curricula engaging industry with due considerations towards current needs.
  • Provide exposure of latest tools and technologies in the area of computer engineering.
  • Promote students awareness on life - long learning and encourage them for positive attitude, self-motivation, and problem solving.
  • Adopt new technology for evaluation, assessment and training.
  • Enrich knowledge and skills of faculty for better learning outcomes.

Program Outcomes

Program Outcomes PO's

  • Basic and Discipline Specific knowledge
  • Problem analysis
  • Design/ development of solutions
  • Engineering Tools, Experimentation and Testing
  • Engineering practices for society, sustainability and environment
  • Project Management
  • Life-long Learning

Programme Specific Outcomes PSO's

  • Ability to repair, maintain, and troubleshoot computer systems by applying knowledge of computer hardware and peripheral
  • Ability to apply programming, multimedia, internet, web, and software engineering principles to solve entry level problems in software domain.

Programme Educational Objectives PEO's

  • To provide solution to the challenging problems by applying computer engineering knowledge.
  • To adapt rapidly changing work environment by engaging in life-long learning and professional development in the field of computer engineering.
  • Communicate effectively, work collaboratively, and exhibit high level of professionalism and ethical responsibility.
